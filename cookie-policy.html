2. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us recognize your browser, remember your preferences, and enhance your experience.Cookies may be "session cookies" (deleted when you close your browser) or "persistent cookies" (stored until they expire or are deleted).

3. Types of Cookies we use

Essential Cookies (Strictly Necessary)

These cookies are required for the website to function properly. Without them, key features like secure logins and shopping carts won’t work.

Examples

  • Authentication and security cookies

  • Load balancing and session management cookies

Performance & Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how users interact with our website, allowing us to improve navigation, content, and performance. We use tools like Google Analytics and Klaviyo analytics to track engagement.

Examples

  • Tracking page visits, time spent, and interactions

  • Identifying website issues and optimizing user experience

Functional Cookies

‍These cookies remember user preferences to enhance your experience, such as language settings and customized dashboards.

Examples

Some cookies on our Services are placed by third parties who provide services on our behalf, such as analytics or advertising. These cookies are subject to their own privacy and cookie policies.
