We are a courageous company, built to bestow small businesses with the advantages of giants

For many years, we spent our time defending and solving for vulnerabilities core to our own ventures.

Our successful retail business was rocked by COVID and restricted from online growth, and decades of tangible intellectual property were destroyed by wildfire. Yet we didn’t stop. Like every entrepreneur, we were relentless.

The journey took us to UC Berkeley and MIT to learn how to architect code and get fluent in the boons of blockchain and AI.

In the end, when we looked up and looked out, we realized we’d built something different, better, and more than we ever quite intended.

These tools are what small businesses need to stop running in place and stepping back — and start growing with the levels of leverage that, until now, have been reserved for enterprises.
