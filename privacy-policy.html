The Prow™ Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 1, 2025
Last Updated: August 1, 2025

1. Introduction

Prow Co. (“Prow,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our websites, use our platforms (including ProwIT™, ProwPay™, and Green Stable Coin™ (GUSDC™)), or engage with us.

By accessing or using our services, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

2. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to personal information we collect online and offline, including through:

  • prowco.ai and its subdomains

  • Our products and services (e.g., ProwIT, ProwPay, Green Stable Coin (GUSDC))

  • Email, phone, and social media interactions

  • Marketing campaigns, surveys, events, and partnerships

This policy does not apply to third-party platforms linked from our services. Their policies govern their practices.

3. Information We Collect

Information You Provide to Us

  • Name, email, phone number, business name

  • Login credentials, profile info

  • Payment/billing information

  • Customer support communications

  • AI/analytics data inputs (e.g., uploaded PDFs, Excel files)

Information We Collect Automatically

  • IP address, browser, device type

  • Usage data (clicks, pages viewed, session duration)

    We may use personal information to:
