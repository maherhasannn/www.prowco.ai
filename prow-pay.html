FAQs

What payment methods can I accept?

Cards and ACH are supported out of the box. You can enable both on invoices and checkout links.

How fast are payouts?

Standard payouts are typically 2 business days. Optional instant payouts are available for an additional fee.

Does ProwPay sync with my accounting?

Yes—Pro and Enterprise plans include auto‑reconciliation so collected payments and fees post to your ledger automatically.

Can I pass on card fees?

Convenience fees and surcharging are supported where compliant. Always review local regulations before enabling.
